In the **Vibrant Decay** aesthetic, enemies aren't just monsters; they are **system errors given form**. They should look like they are being rendered incorrectly by the game engine—flickering, smearing, and leaving trails of "dead pixels."

Here is the tiered hierarchy for the denizens of the Dungeon.

---

## **1\. Visual Identity: The "Glitched" Bestiary**

* **The Look:** High-contrast silhouettes (mostly black or white) with intense neon "bleeding" from their joints or eyes.  
* **The Sound:** Bit-crushed shrieks, mechanical whirring, and the sound of a GPU fan failing.  
* **The Interaction:** When they take damage, they don't bleed; they leak **source code** or **static**.

---

## **2\. The Three Tiers of Corruption**

### **Tier 1: Corrupted Data (The Swarm)**

These are low-health, high-frequency entities that test the player's basic movement and aim.

* **The "Null-Pointer" (Stalker):** A spindly, four-legged creature that looks like a flickering wireframe. It teleports short distances, leaving a trail of pink static.  
  * *Behavior:* It tries to get behind the player. If it hits, it doesn't just deal damage; it "glitches" the player's HUD, making it fuzzy for 2 turns.  
* **The "Buffer-Overflow" (Swarmer):** A floating, pulsating sphere of white noise.  
  * *Behavior:* They travel in packs of 5-8. They don't attack directly but try to surround the player and detonate, dealing AOE damage and slowing the player's movement.

### **Tier 2: Static Horrors (The Elites)**

These require specific tactics and usually drop the "Software" needed for builds.

* **The "Fragmenter" (Heavy):** A massive, blocky humanoid that looks like it’s being viewed through a 144p resolution filter. It carries a "Data-Scrap" hammer.  
  * *Behavior:* It slams the ground, creating a "Dead Zone" (a patch of floor that deals damage over time).  
* **The "Logic-Leaker" (Ranged):** A floating torso with multiple arms that fires "Corrupted Packets" (homing projectiles).  
  * *Behavior:* It stays at the edge of the screen. If it hits the player, it forces one of the player's **Firmware abilities** into a 5-second cooldown.

### **Tier 3: Logic Breakers (The Mini-Bosses)**

These only appear deep in the run or near **Stability Anchors**. They are "Reality-Breakers."

* **The "System\_Admin" (The Stalker):** A tall, faceless entity in a pristine white suit that constantly "smears" as it moves, like a dragging window on an old PC.  
  * *Behavior:* It doesn't have a health bar. You can only stun it to escape. It slowly walks toward the player. If it touches you, the run ends instantly. It is the ultimate "Pressure" mechanic.  
* **The "Seed\_Eater" (The World-Eater):** A massive, ceiling-mounted eye made of golden circuitry and purple tentacles.  
  * *Behavior:* It shifts the layout of the room *while you are in it*. Walls move, floors disappear. You have to fight its "Sub-Processes" (Tier 1s) while navigating a shifting maze.
